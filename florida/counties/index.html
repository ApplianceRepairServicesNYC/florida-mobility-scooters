Counties by Region

Southeast Florida (Miami Metro)

Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach - Florida's most populous region with extensive urban infrastructure and cruise ports.

Tampa Bay Area

Hillsborough, Pinellas, Pasco, Manatee - Major metropolitan area with beaches and retirement communities.

Central Florida (Orlando Area)

Orange, Osceola, Seminole, Lake, Polk - Theme park region with world-class accessibility infrastructure.

Southwest Florida

Lee, Collier, Charlotte, Sarasota - Gulf Coast retirement destination with beautiful beaches.

Space Coast / Treasure Coast

Brevard, Indian River, St. Lucie, Martin - Atlantic coast with Kennedy Space Center and beach communities.

Northeast Florida (Jacksonville Area)

Duval, St. Johns, Nassau, Clay, Flagler - Historic St. Augustine and growing Jacksonville metro.

Panhandle

Escambia, Santa Rosa, Okaloosa, Walton, Bay, Gulf, Franklin, Leon - Beautiful beaches and state capital.

North Central Florida

Alachua, Marion, Sumter, Citrus, Hernando - Home to Gainesville, Ocala, and The Villages.

Florida Keys

Monroe - Unique island chain from Key Largo to Key West.
